33-6203. DESIGNATION OF ATHLETIC TEAMS. (1) Interscholastic, inter

84

collegiate, intramural, or club athletic teams or sports that are sponsored

85

by a public primary or secondary school, a public institution of higher edu

86

cation, or any school or institution whose students or teams compete against

87

a public school or institution of higher education shall be expressly desig

88

nated as one (1) of the following based on biological sex:

89

(a) Males, men, or boys;

90

(b) Females, women, or girls; or

91

(c) Coed or mixed.

92

(2) Athletic teams or sports designated for females, women, or girls

93

shall not be open to students of the male sex.

94

(3) A dispute regarding a student's sex shall be resolved by the school

95

or institution by requesting that the student provide a health examination

96

and consent form or other statement signed by the student's personal health

97

care provider that shall verify the student's biological sex. The health

98

care provider may verify the student's biological sex as part of a routine

99

sports physical examination relying only on one (1) or more of the following:

100

the student's reproductive anatomy, genetic makeup, or normal endogenously

101

produced testosterone levels. The state board of education shall promul

102

gate rules for schools and institutions to follow regarding the receipt and

103

timely resolution of such disputes consistent with this subsection.

104
